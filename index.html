About Me (curriculum vitae)

I start my Ph.D in HPC-AI Lab at National University of Singapore (from August, 2021), supervised by Presidential Young Prof. Yang You.

I spend 12 months as a research intern in Alibaba DAMO Acadmey, I am fortunately to work with Prof. Rong Jin and Shuo Wang

Before coming to Alibaba, I received my M.S. degree in 2020 from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Yu Qiao and Assoc. Prof. Xiaojiang Peng .

My research areas include Resource Efficient AI, Affective Computing, Face Analysis, Noisy Robust Learning and so on.

I am so fortunately to work closely with Prof. Hakan Bilen, Prof. Xinchao Wang, Dr. Bo Zhao, Dr. Zheng Zhu, Dr. Jianfei Yang, and Xiangyu Peng.
